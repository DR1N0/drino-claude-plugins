# Example: 30-Second Coffee Commercial Storyboard

## Project Information

- **Title:** Morning Ritual Coffee - Brand Commercial
- **Duration:** 30 seconds
- **Mood:** Warm, cozy, aspirational
- **Purpose:** Coffee brand commercial for social media
- **Target Platform:** Instagram, YouTube ads

---

## Storyboard Table

| Scene | Duration | Visual Description | Camera | Mood | Transition |
|-------|----------|-------------------|---------|------|------------|
| 1 | 5s | Close-up of whole coffee beans being poured into a ceramic grinder, beans falling in slow motion, rich brown colors | Extreme close-up, slow motion (0.5x speed), static | Anticipation, sensory, premium | Match cut to grinding motion |
| 2 | 4s | Steam rising from fresh ground coffee in filter, wisps creating ethereal patterns in morning light | Medium close-up, slight tilt up following steam, soft focus on background | Aromatic, inviting, peaceful | Dissolve |
| 3 | 6s | Hands (wearing cozy sweater) wrapping around warm ceramic mug, condensation visible on the cup | Close-up, static, shallow depth of field | Comfort, warmth, intimacy | Cut |
| 4 | 8s | Person enjoying first sip while gazing out large window at golden sunrise over city, peaceful expression | Medium shot, slow dolly in toward subject, natural window light | Satisfied, contemplative, aspirational | Fade through black |
| 5 | 7s | Clean product shot: coffee bag centered on rustic wooden table, tagline appears: "Start Your Day Right" | Static, centered composition, studio lighting | Professional, premium, elegant | End |

---

## AI-Ready Prompts

### Scene 1: 5 seconds

Extreme close-up of dark brown coffee beans being poured into a sleek ceramic grinder, shot in slow motion at 0.5x speed. Beans tumble and bounce with rich texture visible, creating dynamic movement. Warm morning light highlights the glossy surface of each bean. Shallow depth of field keeps focus on falling beans while grinder blurs softly in background.

**Keywords:** coffee beans, slow motion, extreme close-up, ceramic grinder, warm lighting, premium, texture, dynamic

---

### Scene 2: 4 seconds

Medium close-up of steam gracefully rising from freshly ground coffee in a paper filter, creating ethereal wisps and patterns. Camera tilts up slowly following the steam's movement. Soft morning sunlight streams from the side creating atmospheric backlighting. The ground coffee is deep brown and richly textured. Background softly blurred showing a minimal kitchen counter.

**Keywords:** coffee steam, medium close-up, tilt up, morning light, backlit, atmospheric, aromatic, peaceful

---

### Scene 3: 6 seconds

Close-up shot of hands wearing a cream-colored knit sweater gently wrapping around a warm ceramic coffee mug. Light condensation visible on the cup's exterior, steam rising gently. Shallow depth of field with hands and mug in sharp focus, background softly blurred showing hints of a cozy interior. Warm color palette with soft, diffused lighting suggesting early morning.

**Keywords:** hands, coffee mug, close-up, cozy sweater, shallow depth of field, warm tones, comfort, morning

---

### Scene 4: 8 seconds

Medium shot of a person in their thirties taking the first sip of coffee while gazing peacefully out a large window. Golden sunrise light floods through the window, creating a warm glow on their face. Person shows a content, contemplative expression. Camera slowly dollies in toward the subject, creating an intimate connection. Modern, minimalist apartment interior visible in soft focus.

**Keywords:** coffee moment, medium shot, dolly in, golden hour, window light, contemplative, peaceful, aspirational, modern interior

---

### Scene 5: 7 seconds

Clean, centered product shot of premium coffee bag on rustic wooden table. Studio lighting creates perfect, even illumination with subtle shadows. Minimalist composition with negative space. Coffee bag features elegant design. Text overlay appears smoothly: "Start Your Day Right" in sophisticated serif font. Professional, high-end commercial aesthetic.

**Keywords:** product shot, centered composition, coffee bag, wooden table, studio lighting, minimalist, elegant, premium branding, text overlay

---

## Notes

**Color Palette:** Warm browns, cream tones, golden morning light, minimal black accents

**Audio Suggestions:**
- Soft acoustic guitar or piano
- Subtle coffee brewing sounds (pour, steam, etc.)
- Gentle morning ambiance

**Brand Message:** This coffee brand represents quality, mindfulness, and taking time for yourself in the morning

**Technical Specs:**
- Resolution: 4K (3840x2160)
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 vertical version for Instagram Stories
- Frame Rate: 24fps for cinematic feel

**Post-Production:**
- Warm color grading throughout
- Subtle vignetting for intimacy
- Sound design with layered coffee sounds
- Brand logo animation at end
